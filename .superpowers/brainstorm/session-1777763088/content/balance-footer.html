Balancing the Scale & Refining the Footer

Ensuring perfect centering and a hardened footer design

1. Correcting the "Left-Heavy" Alignment

To ensure the layout is perfectly centered without any visual drift, we'll simplify the container logic. Instead of nested percentages, we'll use a clean symmetric margin.

A

Symmetric Centering

Use margin: 0 auto with a width: calc(100% - 40px). This ensures an identical 20px "air gap" on both sides, guaranteed.
