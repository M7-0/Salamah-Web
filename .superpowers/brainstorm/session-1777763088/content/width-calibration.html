Solving Width & Horizontal Scale

Ensuring the layout breathes correctly within the phone's narrow width

The Width Problem

Currently, several elements are "fighting" the horizontal boundaries. On a 320px-375px phone, the fixed padding and large font sizes force the content to the absolute edge, making it feel cramped and "terrible."

A

Fluid Content Width

Reduce .container width to 90% or 92% on mobile to ensure a consistent safety gutter on both sides. Prevent any element from "touching the glass."

B

Horizontal Typographic Scale

Aggressively scale down letter-spacing and font-size specifically to prevent text-wrapping on the H1 and Stat numbers, keeping them on one line.

C

Smart Overflow/Breakouts

Allow specific elements (like the mockup frame) to bleed slightly into the margins while keeping the text content strictly centered and scaled.
